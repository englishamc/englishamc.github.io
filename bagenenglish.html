Ananda Mohan College options

Please note that not all optional courses such as DSE and SEC are offered every semester. The ones which are offered are as follows:
Semester 5: DSE A2

Semester 6: DSE B1

For SEC, please note that it is not compulsory to choose an English SEC but if you do so, you need to complete both SEC A and SEC B in English. Similarly, for the other SEC subject that you choose for B.A./ B.Sc. (Gen.), you also need to complete both SEC A and SEC B in that subject.
